Duality: one body, two brains

What if we were able to transport synaptic energy from a living brain to stimulate other remote organic material... perhaps even a clinically dead brain? Could we re-animate this organ at some basic level? Duality is Thrill Laboratory's first step towards answering these questions. Find out more by viewing the video below.

The brain visualisation shown here is being used by our technicians to examine live neuro-data as it streams from subjects watching some of the world's most extreme horror films, courtesy of Mayhem Film Festival. Experiment screening times:

31-10-14 / 22:30 GMT / Texas Chainsaw Massacre
01-11-14 / 14:00 GMT / Starry Eyes
01-11-14 / 16:00 GMT / Housebound
