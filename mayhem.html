Duality: one body, two brains

In the autumn of 2014 Thrill Laboratory presented the live public broadcast of an experiment created to reveal the effect of horror films on the human brain. The experiment was commissioned by Nottingham’s Broadway Cinema for the Mayhem Film Festival. The video below was used to set the context for the audience. Real time data was visualised here, online to be accessed by a remote audience at home, and also projected in the cinema for a local festival audience. The data was also used at the same time to animate an interactive ‘internet of things’ sculpture. The underlying technology has been developed in collaboration with Horizon, to be massively scalable and particularly suited to multi-platform TV broadcast.
